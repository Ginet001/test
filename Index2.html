About RIFU

About Us

The Regional Intelligence Fusion Unit (RIFU) is a specialized security and intelligence coordination body established to facilitate real-time information sharing, strategic collaboration, and joint operational planning among security agencies within a defined geographical region.

RIFU plays a pivotal role in enhancing regional security by integrating intelligence from multiple sources to produce actionable insights aimed at preventing, detecting, and responding to threats such as terrorism, organized crime, banditry, insurgency, and other security challenges. RIFU operates as a multi-agency platform, bringing together representatives from various security, military, law enforcement, and intelligence agencies.

Our Vision

"To be the cornerstone of integrated intelligence for a safer region."

Our Mission

"To coordinate intelligence and strengthen security through actionable data and strategic partnerships."

Core Objectives

Collating and analyzing intelligence from national and regional actors

Facilitating timely dissemination of critical information

Supporting coordinated security operations across boundaries

Building synergy among stakeholders for cross-border security

Enhancing early warning and response systems

Leveraging technology for situational awareness
